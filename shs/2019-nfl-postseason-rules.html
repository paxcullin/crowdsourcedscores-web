2019 NFL Postseason Contest Rules

The Stakehouse Sports 2020 NFL Postseason Contest will run every week of the 2019 NFL Postseason from January 4 through the final game of the NFL season on February 4, 2020.

Official Rules will be posted by 9:00 am PT on January 3.
